Discussion and Confirmation

Posted by Ri Sheng on October 23, 2023

Lesson Week 2 - Start of class

When the class starts, Ms Lee, our lecturer, started off the day with a quick summary on last week's lesson to refresh us on what happened last week and gave us a briefing on the things different roles each member will have when working on the hackaton and during the presentation to show off our project.

We were given a quick brief on what we will be expecting through out the day and we were introduced to inception deck.

Lesson Week 2 - Inception Deck

Interview with Open Government Products Representitive

My team for PFD includes me, Arash, Ching Heng, Darance and Janani. We decided to meet on Sat 21st October to discuss on things we could add and things we can ask our teacher during the next lesson so that we can have a head start compared to other groups.

Our solution to the problem statement, "Closing the Digital Divide for Common Services", is to create a website where we are able to simply and summarise text and langauage in order for people to understand what it means easily. The reason for this is due to many news, email and things we see online might not be as clear and can easily be misunderstood and misinterpreted. The goal of this website is to help simplify text and sentances, mainly people in the elderlys, to help them further understand what the news and emails they receive are telling them.

The first thing we thought of during the group meeting is to use a api from chatgpt to help us with the langauge filtering as it is hard to programme a machine learning algorithm to help filter, simplify and create sentences for people to understand with our given time. So we decided to use chatgpt api to help solve this issue. We are met with our first issue where we need to pay for the usuage of the api and non of us would want to fork out money to for our assignment.

The second idea we have is to make an extension for google chrome. How it works is that when they have the extension installed into their web browser, people will be able to use the extension, copy and paste the sentenaces they want to be summarised and use it easily. The problem we faced is that we are not sure if this is a valid solution and would need to confirm with our lecuturer during the next lesson.

The final idea we have is to make a survey, post it online to ask for ideas, flaws and solution they have faced and thought of so that we are able to think and implement those solutions into our project. The main reason for this survery is to find out the people's needs and the problem they face in order to make our website useful for public daily use.

Summary

In Summary, this lesson have helped me understand the importance of looking at things in two different directions. The developers point of view and the users. Solution the user thought of might not be as easy to implement as what they think as there are always obstacles there to prevent the developers to do so.

I hope during the next lesson, we would be taught on solutions and ways we can use to help us brainstorm new ideas and solutions.
