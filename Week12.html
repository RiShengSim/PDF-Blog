Group Meetings and discussions

Since there is not school today, I called upon my team to have a quick teams meeting to discuss on what we are going to ask cher next week and what we plan to do for assignment 2. Some of us are still in the holiday mood as we still want to procrastinate our work and want to enjoy a bit more before school start. Regardless of those feelings, we gave each other a target for the day which is to find at least one idea each and mention and ask our teacher for advice on what we should be doing next.

Darance & I thought of the idea of making a landing page where we are able to promote our app and make guides in the app. The justification is due to our app is currently hidden and we do not have a tutorial on how to use the app. In addition, we want to show new users reason to use the app and why they should get it.

Next Ching Heng got the idea of adding a theme customisation to add cutsomisable options to help cater to the user preferance and add font sizes so that they are able to see words clearly.

Arash Thought of translation where the user is able to translate the simplified text to other langauages if they are not native to the english language.

After much more discussion, we decided to ask these four questions to our teacher to ask for opinons whether they are good or bad the following week when we have lessons.
